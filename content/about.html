About Kelsey

{{>email-icon}}kelsey@kelseywilson.co {{>phone-icon}}+1.214.803.4038

Kelsey received her B.F.A. in Photography from the University of North Texas, spent some time living and shooting in NYC, and currently lives in her hometown of Dallas. Her studio is located in the neighborhood of Oak Cliff, but she frequently travels for both editorial and advertising clients. Kelsey specializes in food, lifestyle and fashion photography. She lives with her husband, dog and cat in a 1926 brick tudor.
